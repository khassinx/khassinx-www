How we build

Every KhassinX app respects the same architectural principles. Not slogans — design constraints we enforce in code review.

  • On-device intelligence — Apple Foundation Models running locally. Your data never leaves your device for AI inference.
  • Zero tracking — no analytics SDKs, no telemetry, no third-party trackers linked.
  • No account required — apps work the moment you install them. No sign-up, no email collection.
  • One-time payments — when an app is paid, it's a one-time purchase. No subscriptions that lapse.
  • Native Apple Watch support — companion experiences where useful, not just notifications.
  • iOS 26 Liquid Glass — built on Apple's latest design system, not a legacy theme.
