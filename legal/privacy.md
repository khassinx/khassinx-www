**This policy covers `khassinx.com` only** — the umbrella marketing site. Each KhassinX app publishes its own privacy policy on its subdomain (e.g., `asvab.khassinx.com`). For information about how a specific app handles your data, please refer to that app's policy.

## Summary

We do not collect personal data through this website. There are no analytics scripts, no cookies, no third-party trackers, no fingerprinting, and no account system. The site is static HTML served via GitHub Pages with Cloudflare as a network layer for performance and security.

## What we don't do

- **No analytics.** No Google Analytics, no Plausible, no Fathom, no first-party analytics either.
- **No cookies.** The site does not set cookies. Cloudflare may set a single short-lived cookie for security/anti-bot purposes; we cannot disable this but it identifies nothing about you.
- **No third-party scripts.** No social embeds, no chat widgets, no ad pixels.
- **No account system.** There is nothing to sign up for on this site.
- **No data sale or sharing.** There is no data to sell or share.

## What is collected by our hosts

- **GitHub Pages** (the static file server) and **Cloudflare** (the network proxy) automatically log standard HTTP request metadata such as IP address, user-agent, timestamp, and requested URL. We do not have access to user-level analytics from either. These logs are governed by [GitHub's privacy policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement) and [Cloudflare's privacy policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

## Email contact

If you write to us at any `@khassinx.com` address, we receive your message and reply address through our email provider. We retain that correspondence to handle the conversation and any follow-up. We do not add you to any mailing list.

## Your rights

You did not give us personal data through this site, so there is nothing of yours to delete or export here. If you wrote to us by email, you can ask us to delete that correspondence at any time.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the site or our infrastructure changes. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top.
